Hiking

Matheran boasts over 30 scenic hiking trails, each offering its own blend of dense forest paths, panoramic viewpoints, and peaceful solitude. From gentle walks to more rugged routes, every trail here is an opportunity to slow down and immerse yourself in nature.

Live Music

Surrounded by lush greenery and free from the noise of vehicles,think soulful performances, cozy bonfires, and chilled-out moments with friends. Whether you're dancing barefoot or simply soaking in the sounds with a cool breeze in the air, it's the perfect getaway for music lovers looking to relax, reconnect, and vibe in the heart of the hills.

Trekking

Trekking in Matheran is a refreshing escape into nature’s embrace, where every trail is wrapped in lush greenery and peaceful silence. As you walk through red mud paths shaded by thick forests, you're greeted by the soothing sounds of chirping birds, rustling leaves, and cool mountain air.

Forest Camping

Forest Camping in Matheran is the perfect blend of adventure and serenity, where you swap city lights for starry skies and wake up to the sounds of birds and rustling trees. Tucked deep within the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, camping here lets you truly disconnect and recharge in nature’s lap.
